Why make a mask?

The problem is very real

Going out is not an option

COVID-19 spreads fast and has affected people of all ages around the world. When you step out of the house, you may bring the virus back to your children. When you go to buy vegetables, you may make the grocer sick. Your life is so intertwined with the lives of your neighbors that now, as cases in Pakistan rise rapidly, it is your actions that will affect your community. Stay at home. If you absolutely have to go outside, keep yourself and others safe by wearing a mask.

Experts suggest that without a mask, you could spread the coronavirus without showing any symptoms

Not only coughing but simple actions such as breathing and talking can spread droplets into your surroundings. Coronavirus can then latch onto those droplets and make its way to the people around you. Worse, those droplets could stick to that grocery counter and sicken the next person who touches it.

Masks are Effective

Scientific studies show that a simple, homemade face mask reduces the spread of the virus. Countries such as Vietnam, Singapore, and Taiwan are among many others worldwide which have passed mask laws. Masks have also been made mandatory in parts of Pakistan. How to make a mask

Things you Need
  1. Scissors
  2. T-shirt
  3. Tissue paper or clean cloth
Instructions
  1. Cut across the T-shirt in the indicated pattern.
  2. This will form a hollow ‘mask’ with two sets of straps.
  3. Take a piece of tissue paper. A clean square of cloth will also work.
  4. Place the tissue paper between the front and back of the shirt.
  5. The two upper straps will be placed behind your neck and the two lower straps will be placed at the back of your head.
  6. Tie the straps accordingly to form a mask which covers your nose and mouth.

Why make one at home?

Medical-grade masks are necessary for our doctors and nurses who are working tirelessly for our health. By making your own masks at home, you keep professional masks available for medical workers, and you keep them safe as well. A mask is really simple to make at home from any cloth and sewing materials which you own.
